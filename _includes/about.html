About

Temple of the Unticketed is a project to connect Burners on playa with Burners who can't be

The only thing harder than going to Burning Man is not going, but sometimes life gets in the way. Camp Envy and BMIR help but leave homesick Burners no way to reach out and say hello. The Temple of the Unticketed is an art installation on playa populated with images sent from Burners off playa. Particpants use the interactive web experience to take photos, get creative with filters, and then share the results.

Just Enough Blinky

Over five hundred backlit LCD screens are bright without being as in-your-face as pure LEDs. You can see it from far away and still get up close and personal.

Something New

Large scale LED displays are commonplace on playa now. This large scale LCD installation adds a new element to the experience.

Interactive not Intrusive

TotU is one-way so Burners in Camp Envy can reach out and touch someone without affecting anyone's privacy.
