Status

Proof of Concept

We’ve already completed the first proof-of-concept technical prototype for Temple of the Unticketed, and we’re planning on having a larger-scale proof-of-concept finished before the end of the year. We’re currently working with local Burning Man artists to finalize the artistic design, and we plan on starting construction early next year.

You can find all of the designs, code and other work on our GitHub Page.

Budget

Our expected cost range is $8,000 to $10,000 depending on technical factors associated with driving so many high-density LCD displays. We are applying for a Black Rock Arts grant to cover $3,000-$5,000 and plan to cover the rest through additional fundraising and out-of-pocket.
